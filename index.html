About Calabar Night Bites

Born from the vibrant nightlife of Calabar, we bring the authentic taste of the city's street food right to your doorstep. Our platform connects you with the best local vendors who have been serving delicious meals for generations.

We understand the night cravings and the need for quick, delicious, and affordable meals after hours. That's why we've curated a selection of the most popular street foods available for delivery until 3AM.
