Terms of Service

Last Updated: May 2025

1. Order Acceptance

By placing an order through Calabar Night Bites, you agree to our pricing structure which includes:

  • Base food prices set by vendors
  • ₦300 service fee (covers platform maintenance)
  • ₦500 delivery fee (adjusted quarterly for fuel fluctuations)

2. Late-Night Operations

For orders between 10pm-4am:

  • Delivery times may extend by 15-25 minutes during heavy rain
  • We reserve the right to pause service during extreme weather

3. Safety Protocol

Our riders are equipped with:

  • GPS-tracked delivery bags
  • Emergency contact systems
  • Reflective gear for night operations
